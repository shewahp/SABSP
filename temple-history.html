Temple History

Shri Adhishakthi Bhuvaneshwari Adhinatha Siddapeeta has its roots in ancient practices and spiritual traditions. The temple was established by divine inspiration and the vision of our founding guru, who was blessed with direct guidance from the goddess herself.

Over the years, it has grown into a major spiritual center in the region, preserving traditions while engaging actively with the local community. Devotees have reported divine experiences and transformation through prayer and service.
