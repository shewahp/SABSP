Festivals Celebrated

The temple observes all major Hindu festivals with devotion, grandeur, and traditional rituals:

  • Navaratri: Celebrated over 9 days with daily Devi Alankara and cultural events.
  • Deepavali: Temple illuminated with lamps and fireworks.
  • Makara Sankranti: Special pooja and community prasadam.
  • Ugadi: New Year rituals and blessings from the goddess.
  • Annual Brahmotsavam: The grand annual festival with procession and vahana seva.
