Temple Trust

The temple is managed by a registered trust committed to transparency, spiritual service, and cultural preservation. The trust oversees all temple operations, events, construction, donations, and outreach programs.

Objectives of the Trust:

  • Organizing religious festivals and spiritual discourses
  • Providing annadanam (free meals) and medical aid
  • Supporting education and cultural awareness
